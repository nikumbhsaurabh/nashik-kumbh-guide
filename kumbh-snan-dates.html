Kumbh Snan Dates Nashik 2025

Kumbh Mela in Nashik is one of the largest religious gatherings in India. Lakhs of devotees visit Ramkund and Panchavati to take holy dip during Shahi Snan dates. Knowing the correct snan schedule is very important to plan your travel and stay.

Important Shahi Snan Dates (Tentative)

  • First Shahi Snan – July 2025
  • Second Shahi Snan – August 2025
  • Third Shahi Snan – September 2025

These dates are subject to official announcement by Maharashtra Government and Kumbh authorities. Pilgrims are advised to check updated information before planning travel.

Best Time to Visit Ramkund

To avoid heavy crowd, early morning hours are recommended. If you are planning stay near Panchavati, make sure to book hotels in advance.
