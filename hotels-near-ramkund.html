Best Hotels Near Ramkund Nashik for Kumbh Mela 2025

Kumbh Mela in Nashik attracts lakhs of pilgrims from across India. If you are planning to visit Ramkund during the Kumbh festival, choosing the right hotel near Panchavati and Ramkund area is very important.

1. Budget Hotels Near Ramkund

Budget hotels are ideal for pilgrims who want affordable accommodation within walking distance of Ramkund. Hotels like Hotel Sai Yatri and Hotel New Uttam Hira Palace offer rooms starting from ₹1800 per night. These hotels are suitable for families and group travellers.

2. Mid-Range Hotels Near Panchavati

Mid-range hotels provide better comfort and facilities. Hotels like Hotel Panchavati Elite and Hotel City Palace are located within 500–700 meters from Ramkund. They offer AC rooms, restaurant facilities and better amenities for a comfortable stay.

3. Luxury Hotels in Nashik

If you prefer premium stay options, hotels like Hotel Grand Rio and Hotel Express Inn provide luxury rooms, swimming pool, and fine dining. These hotels are slightly away from Ramkund but provide excellent comfort.

Why Stay Near Ramkund?

  • Easy access to Kumbh Snan Ghats
  • Walking distance to Panchavati temples
  • Less travel time during peak crowd days
  • Better safety and convenience

Final Recommendation

During Kumbh Mela, hotels near Ramkund get fully booked very quickly. It is highly recommended to check availability in advance and book early.
